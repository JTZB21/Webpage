Welcome to our definitive ranking list of RPG games that have left an indelible mark on the gaming landscape.

Dragon's Dogma:Dark Arisen

"Dragon's Dogma" is an action RPG set in the fantastical world of Gransys, where players embark on an epic journey as the Arisen, a hero destined to vanquish the dragon threatening the realm. Fueled by revenge, the Arisen sets out on a quest accompanied by a customizable 'Pawn,' an AI companion aiding in battles and progression. The game is renowned for its dynamic combat system, which allows players to climb colossal creatures, engage in visceral battles, and utilize a variety of classes and skills. As the Arisen ventures across diverse landscapes, encounters with mythical beasts,
intricate quests, and a complex narrative unfold, creating a rich and immersive experience filled with exploration,
challenging battles, and a unique pawn system that sets it apart in the RPG genre.

Nioh 2 – The Complete Edition

"Nioh 2" is an action RPG set in feudal Japan, where you play as a half-human, half-yokai warrior on a quest to discover your true identity and battle supernatural forces threatening the land. With intense combat, deep customization, and alliances with historical figures, you'll traverse beautifully recreated environments and face challenging boss battles.
The game's gripping narrative and striking visuals make it a must-play for fans of action RPGs and Japanese folklore.

Witcher 3 Wild Hunt

"The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt" is an open-world RPG where you play as Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter searching for his adopted daughter, Ciri while facing the enigmatic Wild Hunt.
The game's vast world is filled with rich characters, stunning landscapes, and moral choices. Combat, character customization, and deep side quests offer an immersive experience. Expansion packs "Hearts of Stone" and "Blood and Wine" add even more content. It's a must-play RPG, offering a gripping narrative, a beautiful world, and intricate gameplay.

In conclusion, the top three RPG games we've explored offer a diverse range of immersive experiences and captivating adventures, each deserving of their place on this list:


"The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt" reigns supreme with its unmatched storytelling, expansive open world, and deep gameplay. This epic fantasy journey is an absolute must-play, setting a new standard for RPG excellence.

"Nioh 2 – The Complete Edition" takes the spotlight with its blend of samurai action and supernatural elements,providing players with thrilling combat and an enthralling journey through feudal Japan. The addition of the Complete Edition enhances the experience even further.

"Dragon's Dogma: Dark Arisen" stands as a hidden gem among RPGs, offering a unique and dynamic combat system, a richly detailed world, and the allure of tackling formidable mythical creatures. Its blend of action and strategy sets it apart from the rest.

These RPGs, each showcasing distinct strengths and genres, cater to a wide spectrum of gaming preferences. Whether you yearn for the dark and immersive fantasy of The Witcher, the intense samurai battles of Nioh 2, or the adventurous, creature-slaying world of Dragon's Dogma, these games provide an unforgettable RPG journey tailored to your tastes. Dive into these enchanting worlds and embark on epic quests of your own.
